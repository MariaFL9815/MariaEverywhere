Who Am I?

I believe this is a really hard excistential question, but I will do my best...

My name is Maria, born and raised in Buenos Aires Argentina.I enjoy long walks on the beach and a good book...If we replace walks for naps and books for TV shows.

I am too adicted to life. I didn't use to be. I use to stay in all day every day and look so incredibly pale because of it. Now I still do, but ever since I moved to England in 2016, I have found this constat need to grab every single opportunity that gets through at me, and chase the ones that don't. I want to do everything all the time. From undergrad to masters, I have slowly started to learn how to do it all without loosing all of my energy, and ergo the long naps and the netflix.

If anything, this blog is a way of filtering. I have become quite in love with my busy calendar, this blog helps me to sit down and absorve and record my experiences, so I don't forget.
